About us

Zio Orellana (Aesthetician Nurse)

Associates in science in nursing (ASN), Bachelor of science in nursing (BSN), American Board of Surgical Assistant (SAC), Phlebotomy Technician (RPT), and Registered Medical Assistant (RMA). With more than 22 years of experience in full aesthetician, dermatocosmiatry, cosmetology and post-surgical medical therapy. Experience with more than 10,000 patients in different countries. Training countries: Venezuela, USA, Argentina, Colombia, Spain.
